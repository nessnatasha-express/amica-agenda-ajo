Journey: Request a Quote — Prospect & Customer
Objective
Convert a prospective or lapsed customer who has completed a quote into a bound policy. Nurture across email and SMS until purchase, timeout, or suppression.
Journey Type
  • Triggered / Event-Driven — entry fires on a quoteCompleted XDM event from the Amica quote engine datastream
  • Auto, Home, and Life each run as a separate variant sharing this structure
Entry Audience
  • Net-new prospect (no existing policy)
  • Lapsed customer (no active policy >90 days)
  • Segmented dynamically by quoteType: Auto, Home, or Life
Exit Criteria
  • Policy bound (policyBound event received)
  • Journey timeout: 21 days (auto/home), 30 days (life)
  • Prospect opts out or explicitly declines
Re-Entry
Allowed after 60 days on a new quoteCompleted event
Suppressions
Existing active policyholders Incomplete / abandoned quotes Active paid media (suppress 72h post-click) Unsubscribed / global opt-out Do-Not-Contact list
Journey-Level & Channel Caps
Max 1 instance per quoteID Max 2 active instances per profile Email: max 1/day per profile (global) SMS: max 2/week · 9am–8pm local time
Nurture Cadence
Day Channel Message / Goal Trigger Logic
Day 0 Email Quote confirmation + bind CTA Immediate on entry
Day 1 SMS Email Quote reminder with deep link — soft nudge (confirm SMS opt-in) No policy selected in 24h
Day 3 Email Why Amica + proof points — address consideration-stage objections No policy selected
Day 7 SMS Email Quote expiry warning + urgency CTA No policy selected
Day 11 Email Value proposition + quote timing limited — urgency reinforcement No policy selected
Day 14 Email Pre-final follow-up + agent offer — human escalation path No policy selected
Day 23 SMS Email Final follow-up or chat — offer last option, gain insight on decision No policy selected
Day 30 Exit Journey timeout — profile enters lapsed-prospect suppression pool Exhausted after 30 days
